Questions

What is JustOne?

JustOne is a campus-only matchmaking experience that gives you exactly one meaningful match per cycle — no swiping, no endless options.

How is this different from dating apps?

Dating apps optimize for engagement. We optimize for one thoughtful connection. No feeds, no gamification, no distractions.

Who is this for?

Students on participating Indian university campuses who value intention over volume and prefer privacy over visibility.

How does matching work?

Our algorithm considers your questionnaire responses to find the strongest compatibility match on your campus.

Is this anonymous?

Yes. Your identity is only revealed if both you and your match express mutual interest.

What if I'm not matched?

Not everyone receives a match each cycle. We prioritize quality over forcing connections.

When are matches revealed?

Matches are revealed on a set date each cycle, only if interest is mutual from both sides.

Is my data safe?

Your data is encrypted, never sold, and used solely for matching. You can request deletion anytime.

Is this a public launch?

We're starting with a private pilot on select campuses before expanding more broadly.
